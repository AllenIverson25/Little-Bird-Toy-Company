Our Story

Crafting memories, one wooden toy at a time since 2018

The Little Bird Story

Little Bird Toys, established in 2018, brings families handcrafted wooden toys designed to spark imagination and slow down playtime. Our heirloom-quality collection is built from sustainable materials and made to last a lifetime, fostering connections between generations.

We believe in the power of simple, safe play. In a world filled with screens and plastic, we create toys that encourage creativity, imagination, and meaningful family time.

Uncompromising Quality

We believe in the power of simple, safe play. Our handcrafted toys are free from plastic and batteries, and made with sustainable Baltic birch wood.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Little Bird Toys, we are committed to eco-friendly practices. Our wooden toys are designed for longevity, and our manufacturing process minimizes waste and supports the US economy.

  • Zero Waste Manufacturing: Every piece of wood is used or recycled
  • Local Sourcing: Supporting American forestry and craftsmanship
  • Timeless Design: Toys that never go out of style

Master Craftsmanship

Every Little Bird toy is carefully handcrafted by skilled artisans in our Toms River workshop. Using traditional woodworking techniques passed down through generations, we shape each piece with precision and care.

Our craftsmen take pride in every detail, from selecting the finest Baltic birch wood to applying the perfect finish. Each toy undergoes meticulous quality control to ensure it meets our high standards for safety, durability, and beauty.

  • Hand-selected premium wood materials
  • Traditional woodworking techniques
  • Rigorous quality control process
